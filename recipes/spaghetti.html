Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Spaghetti is a cylindrical pasta that is round in cross-section. It is made from durum wheat semolina and water, and is usually white in color. The word "spaghetti" comes from the Italian word spaghetto, which means "little string". Spago means "string" and the suffix -etto means "little one".

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • cheese
  • ground beef
  • sauce

Steps

  1. First, put the noodles into the boiling water
  2. Then, stir the noodles until they're at the level of firmness you prefer.
  3. Next, drain the water from the noodles.
  4. Finish with adding your sauce and stirring it all together until evenly distributed, then sprinkling your cheese on top
