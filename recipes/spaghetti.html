Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • cheese
  • ground beef
  • sauce

Steps

  1. First, put the noodles into the boiling water
  2. Then, stir the noodles until they're at the level of firmness you prefer.
  3. Next, drain the water from the noodles.
  4. Finish with adding your sauce and stirring it all together until evenly distributed, then sprinkling your cheese on top
